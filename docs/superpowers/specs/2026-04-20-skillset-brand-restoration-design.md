# Skillset Brand Restoration Design
## Restore the original identity inside the new multi-page architecture

> **Version:** 0.1.0
> **Updated:** 2026-04-20

---

## What

Restore the essential branding from the original Firebase site into the new Next.js multi-page product shell:

- original logo
- favicon
- white + blue dominant palette
- red as controlled accent only
- previous course and instructor imagery
- trust and social-proof modules

---

## Why

The new site improved structure, routing, and product shape, but it broke continuity with the prior brand identity. The result felt cleaner technically but weaker commercially and less recognizable.

---

## How

Keep the new route architecture, but reintroduce the old brand DNA through a centralized asset layer, updated global tokens, restored homepage authority signals, and image-backed catalog/instructor pages.

---

## Risks

- Reintroducing too much of the old homepage could make the site feel one-page again.
- Old claims around external accreditation should not be reintroduced carelessly.
- Red must remain secondary to blue to avoid visual conflict.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- The new site clearly feels related to the previous Skillset USA brand.
- The product remains multi-page and structurally modern.
- White is the base, blue is dominant, red is accent only.
- Logo and favicon are restored.
- Existing course/instructor image mood is visibly preserved.
