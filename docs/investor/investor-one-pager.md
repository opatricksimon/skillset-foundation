## Company

Skillset is building an international marketplace for serious online courses from independent experts.

---

## Problem

Experts with valuable knowledge still need too many tools to sell structured online education:

- Website or landing page.
- Video hosting.
- Checkout.
- Payouts.
- Community.
- Certificates.
- Course review and trust layer.

This creates operational friction and reduces trust for learners.

---

## Solution

Skillset gives teachers a professional course studio and gives learners a reviewed marketplace experience.

The MVP focuses on:

- Course creation.
- Modules and lessons.
- Video and material upload.
- Paid and free enrollment.
- Stripe Checkout.
- Stripe Connect Express payouts.
- Review before marketplace publication.
- Learner course access and progress.

---

## Market

Skillset sits in the online education, creator economy, and professional learning market.

Comparable product categories include:

- Course marketplaces.
- Creator monetization platforms.
- Learning management systems.
- Infoproduct checkout platforms.

---

## Business Model

Potential revenue streams:

| Stream | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Platform commission | Skillset takes a fee on paid course sales. |
| Creator plans | Paid plans can reduce fees or add professional features. |

---

## Differentiation

- International-first positioning.
- Reviewed courses before public marketplace publication.
- Teacher studio with payout and publishing workflow.
- Verifiable certificates.
- Creator promise around fees, portability, and cancellation.
